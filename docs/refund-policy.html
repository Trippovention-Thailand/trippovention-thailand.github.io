Refund Policy

Our cancellation and refund procedures

1. General Refund Policy

Refunds are subject to the terms and conditions of individual bookings and service providers. Cancellation charges may apply depending on the timing and type of service.

2. Tour Package Cancellations

  • 30+ days before departure: Full refund minus processing fees
  • 15-30 days before departure: 50% refund
  • 7-14 days before departure: 25% refund
  • Less than 7 days: No refund

Note: These are general guidelines. Specific cancellation terms may vary by package and will be communicated at the time of booking.

3. Visa Services

Visa service fees are non-refundable once the application process has begun. Embassy fees may be partially refundable depending on the stage of processing.

4. Flight Bookings

Flight cancellation and refund policies are governed by the airline's terms and conditions. We will assist you in processing refund requests but cannot guarantee refund amounts.

5. Hotel Bookings

Hotel cancellation policies vary by property and room type. Non-refundable rates offer lower prices but no refund upon cancellation.

6. Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed within 7-14 business days. The refund will be credited to the original payment method used for the booking.

7. Force Majeure

In cases of force majeure (natural disasters, pandemics, political unrest, etc.), we will work with you to reschedule or provide credit for future travel.
